## 🧪 About Mod-Sauce

Mod-Sauce is an open-source organization focused on Minecraft mods.  
We build things because they're interesting, useful, or funny in hindsight.

Our mods are held together by **hopes and dreams**.  
Sometimes duct tape.

Primary home is **Codeberg**.  
Mirrors exist. Control does not.

### 🎵 Iam Music Player Renewed
<p>
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-active-success">
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/type-minecraft%20mod-blue">
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/java-17+-orange">

A revived and actively maintained version of the original **Iam Music Player** mod.

Adds real music playback directly into Minecraft.

- Restored core functionality  
- Fixed and updated YouTube support  
- Built for long play sessions  
- Surprisingly works most of the time  

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andreasbm/readme/master/assets/lines/colored.png">
